How Does It Work?

Audio goes in. Video goes out.
(If you want it, audio can go out too.)

[diagram]

Audio input and output jacks are RCA (adapters for XLR or instrument cables are available).

Video output is HDMI (adapters for other video formats are available as well).

How Do I Operate It?

The device itself simply turns on and off, and it includes knobs to adjust the input and output volumes.

The program it runs can work with a standard USB keyboard, a MIDI device, an X Box controller, or any number of custom options--including wireless possibilities!

So, I Can Just Make All This Myself?

Sure!

Every component is open source. If you have the spare time and the desire to learn, go for it!

Of course, you can also record yourself, distribute yourself, manage yourself, book yourself, promote yourself, and pretty much tackle any other aspect of a creative career on your own too. If you actually want to focus on making music, doing everything on your own may not be the best use of your time.
